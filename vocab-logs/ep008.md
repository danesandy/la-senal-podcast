# Vocabulario — Manos a la obra

## Nuevo

- **la obra** — the job/works ('manos a la obra')
- **subir** — to go up, climb
- **bajar** — to go down
- **el cable** — cable, wire
- **conectar** — to connect
- **la caja** — box
- **el apagón** — blackout, power outage
- **mudarse** — to move (house)
- **la entrega** — delivery
- **explicar** — to explain
